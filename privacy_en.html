Cookies can help us determine the pages and content you connect to, the time you spend on specific pages of [Daily Todo], and the services you choose for [Daily Todo]. Cookies is an ID card placed on [Daily Todo]'s computer or server. It can only be set up by their servers to read, and can not execute any code or virus. Cookies enable [Daily Todo] to serve you better and faster, and help you personalize [Daily Todo]. However, you should be able to control whether or not Cookies is accepted by your browser. Please consult the files attached to your browser for more information.
